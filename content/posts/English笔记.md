# 短语表达

- **catch yourself doing something**：to suddenly realize you are doing something
  * Standing there listening to the song, he caught himself smiling from ear to ear.



# Idiom


- **grin/smile from ear to ear**: to look extremely happy
  - "We've had a fantastic response!" he said, grinning from ear to ear.
